Logo for Advertising Company

Skills and deliverables

  • #Adobe Illustrator
  • #Logo Design
  • #Graphic Design

Project description

The client from an advertising company wanted a logo for a name similar to 'ProtoSpace.' They preferred a black base, shades of light blue, white letters for the name, a reference to the location 'In Candeias de Tariva,' and a representation of waves, at least in one of the proposals.

In the first proposal, I created a logo with the initials, utilizing the 'S' to form a wave and provide the client's requested reference.

In the second proposal, I designed something more related to the name itself, a sphere representing the Earth (blue planet) and a spacecraft, giving a nod to the word 'space.'

P.S. The original project had different names, the names currently used are not the original ones.
